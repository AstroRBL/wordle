Quick Rules:

You have to guess the Wordle in six goes or less. Every word you enter must be in the word list. That hasn’t been disclosed, but presumably it’s based on a dictionary. A correct letter turns green. A correct letter in the wrong place turns yellow. An incorrect letter turns gray. Letters can be used more than once. Answers are never plurals.
